ABOUT US

Welcome to Unique Hospital.

Ideally located in the center of Surat city, on the most convenient approached roads, stands the Unique Hospital Multispeciality & Research Institute, a 100-bedded hospital with a 32-bedded well-equipped ICU. Inaugurated for the care of patients on December 30, 2012, the hospital opened its doors for the service of healing with humanity. The hospital is run by Surat Lifecare Pvt Ltd. It was the culmination of a noble desire by Unique Hospital Family, to build a hospital that would gratify the fundamental healthcare needs of the common man with the aim to provide quality healthcare services. The hospital family is dedicated to achieving excellence in healthcare by offering the most modern technology for diagnostic and curative services added up with a world-class ambiance.

OUR MISSION

Multispeciality & Research Institute provides the integral components of world-class care medical & surgical expertise, access to clinical trials and the most STATE-OF-THE-ART treatment approaches, modern, high-tech facilities and compassionate care through dedicated healthcare professionals with humanity in a warm, healing environment.
